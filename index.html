Visit Cairo

Enjoy culture and history of Cairo which is the capital of Egypt and the largest city in Africa, with a name that means "the victorious city.", located on both banks of the River Nile near the head of the river's delta in northern Egypt and has been settled for more than 6,000 years.

Top three activites to do at Cairo

Go on a tour to Gize pyrimads, Memphis City, Dahshur And Sakkara Pyramids

Enjoy a tour of the ancient highlights in and surrounding the city. Admire the Giza Pyramids, see the antiquities at nearby Memphis, and view the age-old Saqqara and Dahshur pyramids

Felucca Sailing trip on the Nile River

Enjoy a River Nile cruise. This Nile River experience takes care of your transfers so you can simply enjoy the ambiance, views, and scenery as you glide across the river in a traditional wooden sailboat called a felucca.

Islamic Cairo Walking Tour: Khan el Khalili, Al-Azhar Mosque

Walk through Cairo’s vibrant Al Muizz Street, browse
the Khan El Khalili Bazaar, and experience Al Azhar Mosque
for an exploration for the city’s Islamic heritage.
Enter Bab El Fotouh gate and take in the medieval
architecture that lines Al Muizz, a street through
the heart of historic Cairo.
